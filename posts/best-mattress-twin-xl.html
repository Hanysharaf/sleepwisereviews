Best Twin XL Mattress 2026 — 7 Picks for Dorms, Adjustable Bases & Tall Sleepers

Reviewed by SleepWise Reviews • Updated May 2026 • 7 picks

The Twin XL is 38 × 80 inches — 5 inches longer than a standard Twin and the exact dimensions needed for a split king adjustable base setup. It is also the standard dorm mattress size at most US universities. We reviewed 7 Twin XL mattresses across three use cases: college dorm rooms (thickness limits, annual moves, budget), split king setups (adjustable base flex, motion isolation), and single tall sleepers in small spaces.
