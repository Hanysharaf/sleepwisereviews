Best Mattress for Studio Apartment 2026 — 7 Picks for Space-Constrained Living

Updated May 2026  |  7 picks  |  Full, queen, and twin XL options covered

A studio apartment mattress has three constraints a standard purchase does not: it needs to arrive through a narrow hallway and stairwell, fit a space where sleeping area shares square footage with living and working space, and — often — do double duty as a sofa or daybed. This guide covers the 7 best mattresses for studio apartments in 2026, with a size-by-room-size guide, delivery logistics, and setup tips for multi-use sleeping areas.

In a studio where the mattress is also visible as a design element, Casper's clean minimal cover design holds up aesthetically. Available in twin, full, and queen.
