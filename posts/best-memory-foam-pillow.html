Memory foam pillows are the most purchased pillow type — and the most misunderstood. The choice between solid and shredded foam, the right loft height for your sleep position, and the question of heat retention all significantly affect whether a memory foam pillow helps or hinders your sleep. This guide cuts through the marketing and gives you seven clear picks with explicit position-based recommendations.

Quick picks: Coop Home Goods Original for the best adjustable shredded foam option. Tempur-Pedic TEMPUR-Cloud for the benchmark solid foam experience. Purple Harmony for hot sleepers who want memory foam support without heat retention.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between solid and shredded memory foam pillows?

Solid memory foam maintains precise shape but cannot be adjusted and traps heat. Shredded memory foam allows loft adjustment by adding or removing fill, allows airflow between pieces (sleeping cooler), and can be sculpted for custom contouring. For most people, shredded foam provides a better practical experience. Solid foam is preferred by those who want consistent support without any shifting.

How high should a pillow be for side sleepers?

Side sleepers need 4-6 inches of loft to fill the space between the ear and the mattress. The exact height depends on shoulder width (broader shoulders need more loft) and mattress firmness. If your neck tilts down toward the mattress, the pillow is too thin. If it tilts up, it is too thick. Adjustable shredded foam pillows are ideal for dialing in the exact height.

Are memory foam pillows good for neck pain?

Memory foam pillows help neck pain by conforming to the neck's curve rather than allowing asymmetric sinking. Cervical contour memory foam pillows are specifically designed for neck alignment. For chronic neck pain, the correct loft for your sleep position is more important than the foam type — shredded adjustable foam allows the most precise customization.

Do memory foam pillows sleep hot?

Traditional solid memory foam pillows trap heat significantly. Shredded foam pillows allow airflow between pieces and sleep noticeably cooler. Gel-infused foam provides limited cooling benefit. For maximum cooling, consider Purple Harmony (latex + grid, no foam) or a shredded foam pillow with a bamboo or TENCEL cover.

How do I know if my pillow height is wrong?

Signs: waking with neck stiffness; numbness in arms; snoring that improves with a different pillow; folding the pillow to get comfortable; chin pushing toward chest (too high) or head falling back (too low). Correct height keeps your ear, shoulder, and hip in a straight line while side sleeping, and maintains the natural neck curve while back sleeping.
