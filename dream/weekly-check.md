# Weekly Check — 30 Minutes

_Every Monday. That's it._

---

## 10-Minute Review

- [ ] Check Telegram daily report — is automation running?
- [ ] Pinterest: impressions up or down vs last week?
- [ ] Any Telegram errors or alerts?

---

## One Task This Week (pick one, rotate)

| Week | Task |
|------|------|
| Week 1 | Update one article in position 5–15 (Search Console) |
| Week 2 | Generate 10 new Pinterest pins → `python automation/scripts/generators/create_week_pins.py` |
| Week 3 | Check Amazon dashboard → log in revenue-log.md |
| Week 4 | Generate one new article → `python automation/scripts/generators/create_articles.py` |

**Rule: one task. Not two.**

---

## Monthly (last Monday of the month, +20 min)

- [ ] Log Amazon earnings in `dream/revenue-log.md`
- [ ] Review `dream/content-gaps.md` — anything changed?
- [ ] Pinterest board check: which pins got the most saves this month?

---

## Escalate to Ravi if

- Pinterest automation has been down 3+ days
- Amazon clicks are 0 for the full month
- An article is flagged by Google (manual action notice in Search Console)
