Editorial Standards

How SleepWiseReviews researches, tests, and rates sleep products — and the rules we follow to keep our recommendations honest.

Independence

SleepWiseReviews is reader-supported. We earn affiliate commissions on qualifying purchases made through our Amazon links. These commissions never influence which products we recommend or how they're ranked. We do not accept:

  • Paid placements or sponsored reviews
  • Brand-funded "best of" lists
  • Free product trades in exchange for positive coverage
  • Editorial input from manufacturers or PR firms

If we cannot recommend a product on its merits, we don't include it. Our full affiliate disclosure is here.

How We Pick Products

Every guide on this site is matched to a specific use case or sleep condition — never generic "best products of the year" lists. For each topic, we:

  • Define the condition-specific criteria. A mattress for sciatica is evaluated for lumbar pressure relief and motion isolation. A pillow for side sleepers is evaluated for loft, neck alignment, and shoulder pressure. These criteria come from clinical sleep research, not marketing.
  • Survey candidate products. We start with 30-50 candidates per topic from the major sleep retailers and shortlist 5-7 finalists for each guide.
  • Apply elimination filters. Products without a clear return policy, products from brands with chronic quality-control issues, and products whose marketing claims contradict the underlying science are dropped.
  • Verify every claim against a primary source. No "studies show" without a study. No physician name without a published work.

Source Hierarchy

Claims about sleep biology, sleep disorders, or product efficacy are sourced in this order of preference:

  • Peer-reviewed sleep journals (Sleep, Journal of Clinical Sleep Medicine, Sleep Medicine Reviews)
  • Position statements from AASM (American Academy of Sleep Medicine) and NIH
  • Books and lectures from board-certified sleep physicians with verifiable credentials
  • Clinical guidelines from Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, Johns Hopkins

Sources are linked or named within each guide. We don't paraphrase research without attribution.

Use of AI

We use AI tools (large language models) during research and drafting. We do not publish AI-generated text without:

  • Human editor review of every product recommendation
  • Verification of every clinical claim against a named primary source
  • Correction of inaccurate or outdated information surfaced by the model

Where a guide includes a passage substantially generated by AI, an editor has reviewed it for accuracy before publication. Where claims cannot be verified against a primary source, they are removed.

Conflict of Interest

SleepWiseReviews has no equity in any sleep brand. No member of the editorial team has a financial relationship with any manufacturer beyond standard Amazon affiliate commissions. We do not own shares in any publicly traded mattress or sleep-product company.

Corrections and Updates

If you find a factual error in a guide, email contact@sleepwisereviews.com. We aim to:

  • Acknowledge correction requests within 7 days
  • Update or retract incorrect statements within 14 days
  • Add a dated correction note when a guide's recommendation is materially changed

Guides are reviewed for accuracy and product availability on a rolling basis. The "Updated" date on each guide reflects the most recent editorial review.

What This Site Is Not

SleepWiseReviews is not a medical resource. Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice. If you have a sleep disorder, chronic pain, or any health condition, consult a board-certified physician. Product recommendations are for general consumer audiences, not patient treatment plans.
