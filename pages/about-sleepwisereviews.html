Short version: Independent publication. No brand affiliation. Affiliate commissions disclosed. Rankings are based on evidence, not commission rates.

What SleepWise Reviews Is

SleepWise Reviews is an independent sleep product publication. We research, compare, and score mattresses, pillows, noise machines, sleep supplements, sleep trackers, and related accessories — and we publish that research in plain language for people who want to make informed purchases, not just follow a sponsored list.

The site launched in 2020 with a simple premise: most sleep product reviews online are either sponsored placements, affiliate-only summaries written from product pages, or press releases dressed up as editorial content. We wanted to build something different — a publication with a documented methodology and a clear policy on commercial relationships.

Who Runs It

The site is operated under the pen name Harry Soul. It is a single-person editorial operation with no brand affiliation, no investor backing, and no advertising sales team. Harry Soul is a privacy preference — it is the consistent editorial voice across all 600+ guides on this site.

If you want to know more about the person behind it, or have a question about how a specific guide was put together, email us. We answer.

How We Make Money

We earn affiliate commissions when readers click our links and make purchases. Our primary affiliate program is Amazon Associates. We participate in other affiliate programs when relevant, and we disclose each one on our Affiliate Disclosure page.

FTC Disclosure: SleepWise Reviews earns a commission on qualifying purchases made through affiliate links on this site. This is how the site is funded. All affiliate relationships are disclosed in line with FTC guidelines. Our affiliate links are placed after rankings are determined — not before.

Editorial Independence

Affiliate commissions do not influence our rankings. We have removed products from top picks when better alternatives appeared, even when the removed product was more profitable for us. A product that earns a higher commission rate does not receive a better score than a product that earns less.

No brand has paid to appear on this site. No brand has seen our scoring criteria before publication. No partner relationship has ever influenced a ranking. That is the policy, and we apply it without exceptions.

Our full review process is documented on our How We Test page.

Our Commitment

  • We update recommendations when products are discontinued, reformulated, or superseded by better alternatives.
  • We note review dates on every guide so you know how current the information is.
  • We are honest about gaps — if we have not found long-term durability data for a product, we say so rather than inventing it.
  • We correct errors promptly and note them in the article when a correction changes a recommendation.

Corrections & Contact

If you spot an error — a wrong specification, an outdated recommendation, a discontinued product we are still listing — email us. We fix corrections promptly and note them in the article.

For product suggestions, methodology questions, or anything else: contact@sleepwisereviews.com
