CONSISTENCY.

Same bedtime. Same wake time. Yes, even on weekends.

Your body has an internal clock (circadian rhythm) that LOVES routine. When you sleep randomly, you're constantly giving yourself jet lag.

Weekend sleep-ins feel good but mess up your Monday. Try keeping within 1 hour of your regular schedule.

This simple habit beats any supplement!
