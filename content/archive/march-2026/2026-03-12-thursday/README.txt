Exercise = Better Sleep... but timing matters! 🏃‍♂️💤

Working out too close to bedtime can actually keep you awake. Your body needs time to cool down.

The science:
- Exercise raises body temperature
- Sleep requires temperature drop
- Allow 3-4 hours between workout and bed

Best times to exercise for sleep:
✅ Morning (ideal!)
✅ Afternoon
❌ Late evening (avoid)
