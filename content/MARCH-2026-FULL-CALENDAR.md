### March 22 (Sunday) - 10:00 AM
**Topic:** Sleep Stages Explained
**Caption:**
What happens when you sleep? 🧠💤

Your brain cycles through stages every 90 minutes:

Stage 1: Light sleep (falling asleep)
Stage 2: Body temp drops, heart slows
Stage 3: Deep sleep (body repair)
Stage 4: REM (dreams, memory processing)

Why this matters:
✅ 7-9 hours = 4-6 complete cycles
✅ Deep sleep = physical recovery
✅ REM = mental recovery

Waking mid-cycle = grogginess!

Try the 90-minute sleep rule:
Sleep in multiples of 90 mins (6hr, 7.5hr, 9hr)
