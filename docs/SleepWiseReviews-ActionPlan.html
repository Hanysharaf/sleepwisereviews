01

05

06

2
PAYONEER — Confirm Payment Setup
Get US bank account details required for Amazon Associates registration
Day 2
01
Log in to payoneer.com with your existing account
02
Navigate to: Receive Payments → Global Payment Service → US Payment Account
This section gives you a US routing number + account number — like having a US bank account
03
Copy and save in a secure note: Bank Name, Routing Number, Account Number
These 3 values are required when registering for Amazon Associates in Phase 3
ℹ️ Note: If the Payoneer account is already verified and you have the US bank details saved, this phase takes under 5 minutes. Verify identity documents are approved if not already done.
3
AMAZON ASSOCIATES — Get Affiliate Links
Apply to Amazon's affiliate program, get your tracking ID, then update all HTML files
Critical — Do Phase 1 First
01
Go to affiliate-program.amazon.com → click "Join Now for Free"
Sign in with an Amazon.com account (US Amazon). Create one if needed.
02
Account info: fill name, address → Website URL: https://sleepwisereviews.blogspot.com
03
Traffic sources: select Search Engines + Social Networks → Monetization: Affiliate links
04
Payment method: US Bank Account (Direct Deposit) → enter the 3 Payoneer values from Phase 2
Bank name, Routing number, Account number — from your Payoneer US payment account
05
Complete the W-8BEN tax form (for non-US residents) — takes about 5 minutes. Enter your name, country (Egypt), tax ID if available
06
Submit application → wait for approval email (usually instant or within 24 hours)
07
After approval: go to Associates Central → Account Settings → copy your Tracking ID (example: sleepwisereviews-20)
08
Open Notepad++ (or VS Code) → open each of the 4 files: index.html, article-weighted-blanket.html, article-magnesium-sleep.html
In each file: Edit → Find & Replace → Find: YOUR-AFFILIATE-TAG → Replace with your actual tracking ID → Replace All → Save
09
Re-upload the updated HTML files to Blogger (repeat Phase 1 steps 03, 07, 08 with the new files)
⚠️ Critical: Amazon requires 3 qualifying sales within 180 days of approval. If no sales occur, the account closes. Pinterest traffic from Phase 5 drives these first sales. Complete Phase 5 quickly after this.
4
CLICKBANK — High-Commission Affiliate Links
Register instantly and add ClickBank links to earn 40–75% commission per sale
Day 4
01
Go to clickbank.com → Create Account → fill name, email, country → activate (instant)
02

Higher gravity = more affiliates making sales = proven product that converts
03

04
In the magnesium article (article-magnesium-sleep.html), add the Magnesium Breakthrough HopLink as an extra "Check Best Price" button at the bottom of the BiOptimizers product card

05
In Blogger Settings → Payment account: enter your Payoneer email address to receive ClickBank payments
ClickBank pays $10 minimum threshold. Direct deposit to Payoneer takes 2–3 business days
💡 Why ClickBank? One ClickBank sale of Resurge (~$40 product at 75%) earns $30. That equals 12-15 Amazon sales combined. Prioritize adding ClickBank links to high-traffic articles.

01

📌 Pinterest is the fastest route to Month 1 income. US buyers search Pinterest for product reviews. Pins get indexed in 24–48 hours. Each new article → 5 new pins → compounding traffic. Month 1: 50–200 visits. Month 5+: 1,000–2,500 visits.
